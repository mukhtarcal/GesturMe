Demo

This demo showcases a hands-free music control experience powered by Google‚Äôs MediaPipe and a custom gesture detection algorithm. Using advanced AI, we detect specific hand gestures to control music playback without any physical buttons. A two-finger swipe to the right skips to the next song, a two-finger swipe to the left goes back to the previous track, and a palm facing the camera pauses the music.

This innovative approach allows you to control your Spotify playlist effortlessly, making it an intuitive, button-free solution for hands-free music navigation.

Intro

Gesture-based control opens the door to a seamless, hands-free experience, starting with music. Imagine skipping songs, pausing, or rewinding your playlist with a simple hand motion—no buttons required. This is just one example of how our technology makes interactions feel intuitive and effortless.

Our app is built to be adaptable, easily expanding beyond music control to become a powerful tool in countless scenarios. From enabling website navigation to enhancing mobile app accessibility, the possibilities are endless. Picture a social media platform where gestures can let you scroll, like, or swipe seamlessly. Our vision for gesture recognition technology has only just begun, and we’re excited to see how it transforms everyday interactions across devices and applications.

Demo

This demo showcases a hands-free music control experience powered by Google’s MediaPipe and a custom gesture detection algorithm. Using advanced AI, we detect specific hand gestures to control music playback without any physical buttons. A two-finger swipe to the right skips to the next song, a two-finger swipe to the left goes back to the previous track, and a palm facing the camera pauses the music.

This innovative approach allows you to control your Spotify playlist effortlessly, making it an intuitive, button-free solution for hands-free music navigation.
